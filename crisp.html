Use the CRISP steps (Context, Role, Instruction, Specification, Performance & Example) to build clear prompts. Fill each step, choose an output style, then click "Build Prompt".

Tell the AI what you do, what the situation is, your goals, and who the audience is.
Give the AI a specific persona or expertise. E.g., "Act as a senior marketing strategist" or "Be a UX designer who specializes in mobile onboarding flows."
Describe what you want done, why you're doing it, and what success looks like.
Describe the format (bullets, slides, paragraphs), length, tone and voice, and any must-have elements.
State success criteria, metrics to hit, what to avoid, and industry standards. Also provide sample content, previous successes, competitor examples, or specific elements to replicate.
