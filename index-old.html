Logan Rath, PhD

I am an academic librarian at SUNY Brockport where I provide research and instruction support to classes in Education, Modern Languages & Cultures, Psychology, and undergraduate Social Work. I am also an adjunct lecturer at the University at Albany where I teach research methods to future information professionals. I believe in active learning that helps students learn the valued ways that information is produced, found, and used. My research interests include information literacy as a social practice, effective online instruction, and how technology is used in education.
