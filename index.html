Logan Rath, MS, MLS

I am an academic librarian at SUNY Brockport where I provide research and instruction support to classes in Education, Modern Languages & Cultures, Psychology, and undergraduate Social Work. I believe in active learning that helps students. I'm also a doctoral student at the University at Buffalo studying Curriculum, Instruction, & the Science of Learning. My research interests include information literacy as a social practice, effective online instruction, and how technology is used in education.
