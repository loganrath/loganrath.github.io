Phonemic Awareness
Use AI to generate rhyming word pairs & tongue twisters for phoneme blending games
AI creates illustrated rhyming cards with phoneme-focused visuals
AI generates voice recordings of rhymes & sound manipulation activities
AI suggests body motions for each phoneme sound (e.g., jump for /j/)
AI designs sound cards arranged in patterns for sequencing activities
Phonics
AI generates decodable texts with specific letter-sound patterns
AI creates letter-sound posters with color-coded blends & digraphs
AI produces audio lessons pronouncing letter sounds & word decoding
AI suggests hand shapes to trace letter forms while saying sounds
AI generates letter formation guides with directional arrows
Fluency
AI creates repeated-reading passages at student reading level
AI makes fluency tracking charts with visual progress indicators
AI generates model readings for students to echo & practice prosody
AI suggests gestures to match expression & emotion in text
AI designs text layouts with pauses marked for phrasing practice
Vocabulary
AI generates word definition cards & context sentence examples
AI creates visual vocabulary cards with illustrations & word families
AI produces word pronunciation guides & vocabulary songs
AI suggests movements or signs to represent vocabulary words
AI designs word webs showing relationships between vocabulary terms
Comprehension
AI generates comprehension questions & story retelling prompts
AI creates story sequence pictures for narrative comprehension
AI produces think-aloud recordings modeling comprehension strategies
AI suggests character actions to dramatize & understand story events
AI designs graphic organizers for plot, character, & setting details
Print Concepts
AI generates materials showing letter names & alphabet sequences
AI creates labeled diagrams of book parts & text features
AI produces alphabet songs & letter-naming practice recordings
AI suggests finger-tracking motions for left-to-right reading
AI designs visual guides showing print direction & page organization
💡 Implementation Tips
  • Pick 1-2 combinations per week to focus on
  • Use AI to generate the content, then adapt for your specific students
  • Rotate modalities to ensure students experience all five throughout the year
  • Combine multiple cells for rich, integrated literacy lessons
  • Save successful AI-generated activities for future use and refinement
🎯 Example AI Prompts You Could Use
Phonemic Awareness + Audio
"Create 5 rhyming word pairs with audio pronunciations for kindergarteners to practice blending sounds together. Include fun sound effects."
Vocabulary + Visual
"Design 10 vocabulary word cards for first graders with colorful illustrations, the word, and a simple sentence. Include related word families."
Comprehension + Gestural
"Suggest 5 body movements and gestures that first graders can use to act out the main events of 'The Very Hungry Caterpillar.'"
Fluency + Audio
"Create a model audio recording of a 100-word decodable passage read with appropriate prosody and pacing for first graders to echo."
